Bidirectional QT Interception

Parameters

Source
Destination
Quantum Drive
This tool calculates the quantum travel intersection point between the OM of one celestial body and the center of another. Specify the source, destination, and model of the Mantis quantum drive (size 1), and the tool will calculate the required distance (Gm) and time travel (sec) to the intersection point. Using voiceattack, you can create some actions related to the travel time to stop the ship.
